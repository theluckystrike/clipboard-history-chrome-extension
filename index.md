# Clipboard History Pro

**Never lose copied text again.** Clipboard History Pro saves everything you copy and gives you instant search and keyboard shortcuts to find and reuse past clips.

How many times have you copied something, then copied something else, and lost the first item? Clipboard History Pro keeps a running history of everything you copy so you can always get it back.

[Install Free from Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/clipboard-history-pro/ddmidpneacclepjmdjibmcdijedgdidf){: .btn }
[View Source on GitHub](https://github.com/theluckystrike/clipboard-history-chrome-extension){: .btn }

## Why Clipboard History Pro?

Your operating system clipboard only holds one item at a time. Every time you copy something new, the previous item is gone. For developers, writers, researchers, and anyone who works with text, this is a constant source of lost work and wasted time.

Clipboard History Pro extends your clipboard into a searchable history. Copy as many items as you need, then find any of them instantly with search or keyboard shortcuts.

---

## Key Features

- **Automatic clipboard tracking** -- Every text item you copy is saved to your local history
- **Instant search** -- Type to filter through your clipboard history in real time
- **Keyboard shortcuts** -- Access your clipboard history without touching the mouse
- **One-click paste** -- Click any item to copy it back to your clipboard
- **Pin important items** -- Keep frequently used text clips at the top of your history
- **History limit control** -- Configure how many items to keep (default: 500)
- **Category organization** -- Clips are automatically organized for easy browsing
- **Dark mode** -- Follows your system preferences for comfortable viewing
- **Export history** -- Save your clipboard history for backup or migration
- **Multilingual** -- Available in English, Spanish, and Japanese

## Privacy and Security

Your clipboard can contain sensitive information -- passwords, personal data, private messages. Clipboard History Pro keeps all of that safe:

- All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage
- Nothing is transmitted to any external server
- No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry
- You can delete individual items or clear your entire history at any time
- Full source code available on [GitHub](https://github.com/theluckystrike/clipboard-history-chrome-extension)

## How It Works

1. **Install** the extension from the Chrome Web Store
2. **Copy text normally** -- Clipboard History Pro runs in the background
3. **Click the icon** or use the keyboard shortcut to open your history
4. **Search or browse** to find the item you need
5. **Click any item** to copy it back to your clipboard and paste it anywhere

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does Clipboard History Pro save images or files?
Currently, Clipboard History Pro focuses on text-based clipboard items. Images and file references are not tracked.

### Is my clipboard data sent to any server?
No. All clipboard history is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. No data ever leaves your machine.

### How many items can it store?
By default, Clipboard History Pro keeps the last 500 items. You can adjust this limit in the extension settings. Older items are automatically removed when the limit is reached.

### Can I use keyboard shortcuts to access my history?
Yes. Clipboard History Pro supports configurable keyboard shortcuts for opening the history panel and navigating through recent items.

### Will it slow down my browser?
No. Clipboard History Pro uses Chrome's efficient event-based architecture. It listens for copy events and stores text in the background with minimal resource usage.
